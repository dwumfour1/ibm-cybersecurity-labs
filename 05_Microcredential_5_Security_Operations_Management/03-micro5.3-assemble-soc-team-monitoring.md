# Micro 5.3: Assemble a SOC Team and Perform Security Monitoring Tasks

## About This Learning Activity
In this capstone project, you will apply your knowledge of **security operations and monitoring** in a practical scenario.

You will:

- Analyze the security operations requirements of different organizations  
- Assign appropriate roles to members of a **Security Operations Center (SOC)** team  
- Perform **network reconnaissance** to identify systems and potential vulnerabilities  

---

## Prerequisite Learning
This project builds on the knowledge and skills from:

- Micro 5.1: Security Operations  
- Micro 5.2: Security Monitoring  

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, I am able to:

1. Distinguish between different security operations models  
2. Assign SOC roles and responsibilities based on organizational needs  
3. Perform network reconnaissance to support security monitoring  

---

## Assessment Details
You will complete **two assessments** in this capstone project:

1. **Project Questions:**  
   Demonstrate your understanding of security operations and management. A score of at least 80% is required, with multiple attempts allowed.

2. **10-Question Assessment:**  
   Test your applied knowledge and skills. A minimum score of 80% is required, with feedback and retry options available.

---

## Reflection
This capstone project allowed me to combine knowledge of **SOC structure and security monitoring** into a practical scenario. Assigning roles and performing reconnaissance helped me understand how security teams operate in real-world environments.  

Documenting this process strengthens my ability to **support security operations and contribute effectively within a SOC environment**.
