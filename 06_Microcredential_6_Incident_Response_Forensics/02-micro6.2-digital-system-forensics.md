# Micro 6.2: Digital System Forensics

## About This Learning Activity
In this module, I explored the fundamentals of **digital forensics** and how investigators collect, preserve, and analyze digital evidence after a cyber incident.

This module focused on understanding how forensic investigations support incident response and legal processes.

Key areas covered include:

- Sources and uses of **digital forensic evidence**  
- Legal considerations such as **chain of custody**  
- The phases of the **digital forensics process**  
- Tools and techniques used to analyze digital evidence  

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, I am able to:

1. Analyze a cyberattack using digital forensics techniques  
2. Apply the four phases of the digital forensics process to a scenario  
3. Analyze digital forensic evidence effectively  

---

## Lessons

- Introduction to Digital Forensics  
- The Digital Forensics Process  
- Digital Forensics Tools  

---

## Reflection
This module helped me understand how digital evidence is collected and analyzed in a structured and legally sound way.  

Learning about the **chain of custody** and forensic phases showed me how important accuracy and integrity are when investigating cyber incidents.  

This strengthens my ability to support investigations and understand what happened during a security breach.
