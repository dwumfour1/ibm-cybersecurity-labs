# Micro 6.3: Investigate an Incident Through Digital Forensics

## About This Learning Activity
In this capstone project, I applied incident response and digital forensics concepts to investigate a simulated cybersecurity incident involving Oceanix Living, a manufacturing company affected by a data compromise.

This project focused on responding to the incident efficiently while conducting a structured forensic investigation to preserve and analyze digital evidence.

Key areas covered include:

- Applying an incident response framework to a real-world scenario  
- Conducting forensic investigations after a security breach  
- Preserving the integrity of digital evidence  
- Analyzing forensic data to understand the incident  

---

## Prerequisite Learning
This project builds on the knowledge and skills from:

- Micro 6.1: Incident Response  
- Micro 6.2: Digital System Forensics  

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, I am able to:

1. Investigate incident reports using digital forensics techniques  
2. Create a USB flash drive image for forensic analysis  
3. Analyze digital forensic evidence to support incident investigations  

---

## Assessment Details
You will complete two assessments in this capstone project:

1. **Project Questions:**  
   Demonstrate knowledge of incident response and digital forensics. A score of at least 80% is required, with multiple attempts allowed.

2. **10-Question Assessment:**  
   Evaluate applied knowledge and skills from the project. A minimum score of 80% is required, with feedback and retry options available.

This capstone project strengthened my understanding of how cybersecurity professionals investigate and respond to security incidents in real-world environments.
