# Emerging Threats and the Future of Cybersecurity Technologies

## About This Learning Activity
In this module, I explored how emerging technologies are transforming cybersecurity and creating both new opportunities and new risks.

As technology continues to evolve, organizations must balance innovation with strong security practices to reduce cyber threats and protect critical systems and data.

Key areas covered include:

- Understanding emerging cybersecurity threats associated with modern technologies  
- Exploring how new technologies can strengthen or weaken cybersecurity  
- Examining the future direction of cybersecurity technologies and defense strategies  
- Understanding how organizations adapt security practices to evolving digital environments  

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, I am able to:

1. Summarize emerging cybersecurity threats  
2. Describe the future of cybersecurity technologies  
3. Explain the impact of emerging technologies in cybersecurity  

---

## Topics

- Artificial Intelligence (AI)  
- Internet of Things (IoT)  
- 5G Networks  
- Quantum Computing  
- Biotechnology Enhancements  

---

## Reflection
This module expanded my understanding of how rapidly evolving technologies influence cybersecurity.  

I learned that while technologies such as AI, IoT, and quantum computing provide powerful capabilities, they also introduce new attack surfaces and security challenges.  

Understanding these emerging threats helps me think more strategically about the future of cybersecurity and the importance of adapting security practices as technology evolves.
