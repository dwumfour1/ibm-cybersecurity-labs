In this final assessment, I demonstrated my understanding of the key concepts and skills covered throughout the IBM SkillsBuild Cybersecurity learning path.

- Unlimited attempts are allowed  
- Each attempt must be completed in one sitting  
- The browser window must remain open throughout the attempt
