Welcome! In this module, I will guide you through the foundations of cybersecurity, focusing on **data and privacy**. By the end, you’ll understand how organizations protect sensitive information, evaluate security risks, and apply practical controls like encryption and backups.

## Learning Objectives
By following this module, you will be able to:
1. Understand how the three key elements of cybersecurity (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability) apply to real tasks
2. Identify and categorize the types of sensitive data organizations must protect
3. Apply the CIA triad to real-world situations
4. Learn how to encrypt files and drives
5. Develop a practical data backup plan

## Lessons We’ll Cover
We will explore:
- Introduction to cybersecurity
- Data privacy fundamentals
- Data security controls
- File and drive encryption
- Data backup planning

## Key Points to Focus On
- Think critically about **why data must be classified and protected** — it’s not just rules, it’s about real-world consequences.  
- Pay attention to **encryption and backups** — these are simple but highly effective security measures.  
- Always connect theory (CIA triad) to **practice** — imagine how you would protect a small organization.

## Reflection
After completing this module, I feel confident in understanding how sensitive data is protected. I can explain the CIA triad in practical terms, apply encryption to secure files, and plan a backup strategy that would work in a real-world setting.
